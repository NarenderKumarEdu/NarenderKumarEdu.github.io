The Person Behind the Research

About Me

Bridging the gap between educational theory and the reality of the classroom.

Hello, I am Narender Kumar.

I am an independent researcher and educator based in Haryana, India. Currently working as a Computer Science PGT, I have a front-row seat to the challenges and triumphs of the modern school system.

While I teach technology, my research passion lies in the human side of education. I study how teaching methodologies impact student psychology, engagement, and long-term learning outcomes. My goal is simple: to find evidence-based ways to make schools better for both students and teachers.

"The quality of an education system cannot exceed the quality of its teachers."

My Research Focus

I don't just theorize; I observe. My work focuses on three key areas:

  • Quality of Education: Measuring real learning outcomes beyond just exam scores.
  • Pedagogical Methods: Developing teaching styles that combat passivity and encourage critical thinking.
  • Teacher Psychology: Understanding the pressures on educators and how to support them.
